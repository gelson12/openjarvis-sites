Our Family Story

For over five decades, Aurelio & Sons has been a beacon of Italian culinary tradition in the heart of London's vibrant Soho. Founded by Aurelio Senior, a passionate chef from Naples, our trattoria was built on the simple philosophy of bringing authentic Italian home cooking to our cherished guests.

Every dish tells a story, passed down through generations. From our grandmother's secret ragu recipe to the artisanal pasta crafted by hand, we honor the legacy of our ancestors. We believe in fresh, seasonal ingredients, warm hospitality, and creating a memorable dining experience that feels like coming home.

Today, Aurelio Junior and his children continue this proud tradition, inviting you to share in our family's love for good food, fine wine, and joyful company. Benvenuti a tutti!
