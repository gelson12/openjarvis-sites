Our Story: A Legacy of Flavor

Aurelio & Sons is more than just a trattoria; it's a living legacy born from the passion of Aurelio Rossi, who brought his family's cherished recipes from the sun-drenched hills of Tuscany to the vibrant streets of Soho over two decades ago.

Every dish tells a story, every ingredient is carefully selected, and every guest is welcomed like family. We believe in the power of food to bring people together, creating unforgettable moments around a table filled with warmth, laughter, and authentic Italian taste. Our commitment to tradition and quality ensures a dining experience that feels like a warm embrace from nonna herself.
