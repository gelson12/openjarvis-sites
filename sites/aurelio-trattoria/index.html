Our Family's Legacy

Aurelio & Sons: Where Every Dish is a Memory

The story of Aurelio & Sons began over three decades ago, when Aurelio Rossi, a passionate chef from Tuscany, brought his family's cherished recipes to the vibrant streets of Soho. What started as a small, humble trattoria, fueled by a love for authentic Italian cuisine, has blossomed into a beloved culinary landmark.

Today, his sons, Marco and Giovanni, proudly carry on his legacy. They continue to uphold the traditions their father instilled: using only the freshest, seasonal ingredients, preparing every dish with heartfelt care, and ensuring every guest feels like family. At Aurelio & Sons, you're not just dining; you're becoming a part of our rich Italian heritage.
